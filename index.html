About Steal a Brainrot

Steal A Brainrot offers players a rather unusual tycoon experience. You have to buy or steal various brainrot characters so that they grind money in your base. And some of them are extremely rare. This guide tells you about all the secret brainrots and how to get them in Steal A Brainrot.
