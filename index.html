About Steal Brainrot Online

Steal Brainrot Online is a chaotic multiplayer heist game where you collect and protect rare Brainrot memes like Tralalero Tralala and Cappuccino Assassino. Buy memes from the feed, sneak into rival bases to steal theirs, and defend your own with traps and locks.

Use boosters, outbid opponents mid-delivery, and rebirth for bigger bonuses as you climb to meme-collecting supremacy.

This is an alternative version of the game by the name "Steal a Brainrot" which is on Roblox.
